Educator Policies

Must demonstrate strong competence in the subject areas they have applied for.

Must be available to hold sessions at given times, at least twice a week during the allocated timings and dates.

Should be respectful and inclusive at all times, ensuring that proper as well as preferred language is used consistently throughout the sessions.

Need to be punctual and regular.

Fulfill the responsibilities given by the admin team.

Should exhibit good usage of technology, inclusive of designing interactive lessons online.

Students Policies

Must set up their technology well in advance of the classes.

Must be punctual.

Must be from the ages of 7-12.

Must be respectful towards fellow peers in the group as well as the educators.
